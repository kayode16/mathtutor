Privacy and Cookie Notice

Emivant Online Mathematics Tutor collects information submitted by parents or guardians through the assessment form, including contact details, country, a child’s age range, curriculum and the Mathematics support requested.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to assessment requests, recommend appropriate tutoring support, discuss lesson availability and manage enrolment enquiries.

Analytics and advertising cookies

With your permission, this website uses Google Analytics and Meta Pixel to understand website activity and measure enquiries. Optional tracking is not enabled until you choose “Accept cookies”. You may reject optional cookies and continue using the website.

Children’s information

Parents and guardians should submit only information needed for the tutoring enquiry and should not submit passwords, financial information, medical records or other unnecessary sensitive information.

Form processing

Assessment submissions may be processed and stored by the website hosting provider, Netlify, so that Emivant can receive and respond to enquiries.

Managing your cookie choice

You can clear the saved cookie choice for this website and choose again.
